About me

I earned my college degree with a Double Major in Health Science and Kinesiology. While I accomplished this, I realized that it wasn't the path I wanted to pursue, leaving me feeling a bit lost. Fortunately, my sister suggested trying the Google Data Analytics course, and to my delight, I found a true passion for it. Currently, I am dedicated to furthering my education by working towards a Computer Science/Data Science Degree. Along the way, I've earned valuable certifications, including the prestigious Google Data Analytics Certificate, the Mimo SQL Certificate, and the Alex The Analyst bootcamp Data Analyst Certificate. As a first-generation Latino with parents who didn't have access to formal education, I am determined to continually improve myself each and every day. My background and experiences inspire me to strive for excellence and make the most of every opportunity that comes my way.
